Source Code of This Site

Weaving stories into motion, I breathe life into frames, ensuring rhythm, emotion, and purpose guide every scene. From the first spark of an idea to the final render, I balance artistic expression with technical mastery, sculpting narratives that resonate.

My journey spans both 2D and 3D animation, though 3D is my true canvas. From concept art to storyboarding, modeling to texturing, lighting to final composition, I orchestrate every step. Each frame is a brushstroke, each movement a heartbeat, shaping worlds where imagination takes flight.

Reality bends at my fingertips as I sculpt fire, weave light, and summon winds of particles into existence. Through simulations, compositing, and procedural magic, I infuse stories with cinematic spectacle.

Specializing in cloth and hair simulation, dynamic lighting, rendering, and compositing, I craft illusions that feel tangible. Whether it's shattering structures, swirling embers, or cascading fabrics, I strive to blur the line between the real and the imagined—painting motion with physics, coding chaos into beauty.

Where art meets technology, I bridge the gap, forging tools, shaders, and pipelines that breathe efficiency into creativity. My hands sculpt not only visuals but the very process itself, streamlining workflows and unlocking new realms of possibility.

With expertise in pipeline automation, procedural modeling, rigging, and texturing, I craft efficiency with Python, VEX, and MEL inside Houdini and Maya. Each tool is a key, each script a bridge—guiding artists from vision to execution with seamless precision.

In the language of logic, I craft order from chaos, building systems that function with precision and intent. Every keystroke is a step toward efficiency, whether refining workflows, automating tasks, or sculpting digital experiences.

Python is my tool of choice, a silent assistant that simplifies time management, sharpens productivity, and shapes custom tools to streamline my creative process. In every line of code, there is a purpose—to create, to enhance, to empower.

There is a world that begins with an idea, and I shape that idea into play. Every mechanic is a pulse, every interaction a story, inviting players to engage, explore, and discover.

Experimenting with different engines, I’ve dabbled in pixel-art platformers, interactive puzzles, and physics-based mechanics. Godot has been a canvas for prototyping unique gameplay ideas, while Unity is where I continue to explore game physics and mechanics, refining my understanding of interactive design. In Unreal Engine, I craft stunning landscapes, molding light with Lumen to create breathtaking, immersive worlds.

A digital space is more than pixels and code—it is an experience, a conversation, an invitation. I design not just with aesthetics in mind, but with purpose, ensuring seamless functionality and intuitive interaction.

The very site before you is crafted by my hands, built from scratch to be an extension of my creativity. Every element, every transition, every detail—designed, programmed, and polished to reflect the essence of who I am.

About Me

Exploring into the world of 3D has always been a passion to me. I like collecting information and knowledges, figuring out how to achieve something really tricky and find out solutions. Every time I figure out the answer, it's just like a Eureka moment and can really make me fulfilled.

Currently I'm working as a volunteer VFX artist in Crash Landing Studio, where I manipulate nodes and codes in Houdini and build seamless pipelines between Blender and that.

I'm also a fast learner, and learning more and more is a lifetime topic to me.
